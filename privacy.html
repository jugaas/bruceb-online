Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-06

Overview

Bruce Games respects the privacy of players, families, and visitors. We minimize data collection and store only what is necessary to operate programs and provide services.

Information We Handle

  • Visit data: Booking and reservation information needed to manage visits and group bookings.
  • Contact messages: Information submitted via contact forms to respond to inquiries from players or teams.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for site functionality and analytics. We do not use tracking cookies targeted at children.

Your Choices

  • Contact us to review or delete personal data related to a booking or inquiry.
  • Opt out of marketing communications by following unsubscribe instructions in emails.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or data handling, please use the contact form on the Home page or reach out to our support team.
