Designing Gamer-Friendly Activity Layouts

Create activity spaces that encourage exploration, safety, and sustained engagement.

Published 2026-01-06

Thoughtful layouts reduce congestion, keep activities safe, and help facilitators manage flows.

Open sightlines

Arrange stations so staff can supervise easily while players explore independently.

Start simple

Begin with low-friction tasks to build confidence before introducing more complex steps.

Clear cues

Use signs, colors, and staff prompts to guide choices and support on-the-spot learning.
