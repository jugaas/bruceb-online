Designing Game Flow & Engagement

Create rhythms that match players' attention spans and support easy transitions.

Published 2026-01-06

Structure game flow with short, engaging levels and clear transitions to maintain players' interest.

Natural breaks

Create clear pause points so players can rest or regroup without losing momentum.

Save progress

Where relevant, keep simple markers of completion so players feel their achievements are remembered.

Return prompts

Friendly reminders and visible milestones encourage further exploration during the session.
