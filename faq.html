FAQ

Answers to common questions about Bruce Games.

What ages are suitable for attending?

The arcade offers activities and services for all audiences; some zones and activities may have age restrictions or recommendations for accompanied minors.

How do I buy tickets?

Tickets can be purchased online or at the box office, subject to availability. We offer packages for groups and teams with special rates.

Are there options for groups or team visits?

Yes. We have programs for teams and guided tours for groups. Contact our team to coordinate dates and gaming activities.

Is the venue accessible?

We have adapted access and staff who can assist visitors with special needs. Please inform us in advance to coordinate specific assistance.

What security measures are in place?

We apply strict security protocols, access control, and have staff trained in emergencies and first aid.
